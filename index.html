Market Entry for a Web3 Company into Europe
(MiCA & ESG Strategy)
Web3 公司进入欧洲市场
（MiCA 合规与 ESG 策略）

Supporting a Web3 company in navigating EU market entry through MiCA compliance, ESG positioning, and localized go-to-market strategy.

帮助一家 Web3 公司通过 MiCA 合规、ESG 定位与本地化 GTM 策略进入欧洲市场。

Challenge
挑战

The client aimed to expand into Europe but faced multiple structural challenges:

客户希望进入欧洲市场，但面临多重结构性问题：

  • Unclear regulatory pathway under MiCA frameworkMiCA 框架下合规路径不清晰
  • Lack of credibility and trust among EU users在欧洲用户中缺乏信任与认知
  • Messaging misaligned with European market expectations品牌叙事与当地市场预期偏差
  • Absence of ESG narrative缺乏 ESG 叙事
Approach
方法
1. Regulatory & Market Framing
1. 合规与市场路径
  • Interpreted MiCA regulatory landscape梳理 MiCA 监管框架
  • Identified viable entry routes设计可行进入路径
  • Mapped risk vs speed trade-offs平衡合规风险与进入速度
2. Positioning & ESG Narrative
2. 品牌定位与 ESG 叙事
  • Shifted narrative: product-driven → trust-driven从"产品导向"转向"信任导向"
  • Introduced ESG layer构建 ESG 叙事
  • Built institutional-friendly messaging优化面向机构的沟通方式
3. Go-to-Market Strategy
3. Go-to-Market 策略
  • Designed channel strategy设计渠道结构
  • Structured phased launch分阶段进入
Outcome
成果
  • Defined regulatory positioning aligned with MiCA明确 MiCA 下的合规定位
  • Improved credibility in EU提升在欧洲市场的信任度
  • Established ESG narrative as differentiation建立 ESG 作为差异化优势
  • Enabled structured GTM execution支撑 GTM 的系统化执行
Insight
洞察

In Web3 market entry, compliance is no longer a constraint — it is part of the positioning.

在 Web3 的市场进入中，合规不再只是约束条件，而是品牌定位的一部分。

Fashion / Retail
Fashion Brand China Market Entry
(Xiaohongshu KFS Strategy)
时尚品牌中国市场进入
（小红书 KFS 全链路策略）

Designing a full-funnel Xiaohongshu strategy for a Western fashion brand's China entry — from KOL seeding architecture to paid amplification and live-stream conversion.

为一家西方时尚品牌进入中国市场设计完整的小红书全链路策略——从 KOL 种草矩阵到付费放大，再到直播转化收割。

Challenge
挑战

The brand faced structural barriers that a Western DTC playbook could not solve:

品牌面临西方 DTC 打法无法解决的结构性障碍：

  • Low brand awareness among Chinese Gen Z consumers在中国 Z 世代用户中品牌认知度近乎为零
  • No established content presence or trust on Xiaohongshu在小红书缺乏内容积累与用户信任基础
  • KOL strategy and budget allocation logic entirely undefined达人策略与预算分配逻辑完全空白
  • Content, paid traffic, and conversion layers disconnected内容种草、付费投流与转化环节相互割裂
Approach
方法
1. Audience & Platform Positioning
1. 用户洞察与平台定位
  • Defined core audience segments and lifestyle scenes on Xiaohongshu明确小红书核心目标人群画像与生活场景
  • Positioned brand within the accessible-premium footwear category将品牌定位于「轻奢运动鞋」赛道
  • Mapped content themes to conversion-relevant moments将内容主题绑定至高转化潜力场景
2. KOL Matrix Design
2. KOL 矩阵设计
  • Structured a 3-tier KOL matrix across head, waist, and long-tail creators构建头腰尾三层达人矩阵，共 122 位
  • Prioritised niche-community fit and engagement quality over follower count以圈层匹配度与互动质量为核心筛选标准，而非粉丝量
  • Developed content briefs preserving authentic creator voice制定保留真实感与创作者个性的内容 Brief 框架
3. KFS Full-Funnel Strategy
3. KFS 全链路闭环
  • Connected organic seeding → Juguang paid amplification → Xiaohongxing search capture → DLive live-stream conversion打通有机种草 → 聚光付费放大 → 小红星搜索截流 → DLive 直播转化收割的全链路
  • Designed phased budget allocation model across RMB 1M envelope设计 100 万预算的分阶段动态分配模型
  • Built optimisation decision framework based on real-time signal monitoring建立基于实时信号监控的优化决策框架
Outcome
成果
  • Clear positioning within the accessible-premium footwear category on Xiaohongshu在小红书轻奢运动鞋赛道建立清晰的品牌定位
  • Structured KOL matrix of 122 creators across 3 tiers with content briefs构建覆盖三层级、122 位达人的 KOL 矩阵与配套内容框架
  • Integrated KFS strategy linking seeding through to live-stream conversion建立从种草到转化的 KFS 一体化闭环策略
  • Data-driven optimisation framework enabling adaptive budget deployment形成支撑动态预算调配的数据驱动优化框架
Insight
洞察

On Xiaohongshu, content density is not a metric — it is a prerequisite. Paid amplification only works when organic trust has already been built.

在小红书，内容密度不是一个指标，而是一个前提条件。付费放大只有在有机信任已经建立之后，才能真正发挥乘数效应。

Cross-border
Asian Perfume Brand
European Market Entry Strategy
亚洲香水品牌
欧洲市场进入策略

Building European brand presence from zero for an Asian perfume house — through distribution, trade partnerships, cultural events, and media seeding in the world's most narrative-driven fragrance market.

帮助一家亚洲香水品牌在零认知基础上进入欧洲市场——通过渠道建设、贸易合作、文化活动与媒体种草，在全球叙事要求最高的香水市场建立真实存在感。

Challenge
挑战

Entering Europe's fragrance market without a distribution network, press relationships, or brand narrative:

在没有分销网络、媒体关系与品牌叙事的情况下进入欧洲香水市场：

  • Zero brand recognition in European fragrance circles在欧洲香水圈中品牌认知度为零
  • No existing relationships with distributors, boutiques, or press与分销商、精品店、媒体均无既有关系
  • Fragrance narrative not adapted to European sensibility香水叙事尚未针对欧洲审美进行本地化
  • No physical or institutional presence in the market缺乏实体存在感与机构层面的可见度
Approach
方法
1. Distribution & Trade Network
1. 渠道与贸易网络搭建
  • Identified and approached relevant distributors, factories, and niche boutiques筛选并接触分销商、工厂合作伙伴与小众精品店
  • Mapped multi-brand retail channels (concept stores, parfumeries) as priority entry points以多品牌零售渠道（概念店、香水精品店）为优先进入节点
  • Structured first-contact outreach with brand positioning narrative以品牌定位叙事为核心，设计首次接触的沟通框架
2. Press & KOL Seeding
2. 媒体与 KOL 种草
  • Designed targeted sample-sending programme for fragrance-focused KOLs and press设计面向香水垂类 KOL 与媒体的定向小样寄送方案
  • Crafted editorial narrative positioning the brand within the niche perfumery movement构建将品牌嵌入小众香水运动的编辑叙事
  • Prioritised authentic community over mass reach以真实社群影响力优先于大众触达规模
3. Brand Activations & Collaborations
3. 品牌活动与跨界联名
  • Structured participation in Paris Perfume Week as the primary institutional visibility moment以参加巴黎香水周作为建立机构层面可见度的核心节点
  • Developed fashion × fragrance co-launch strategy with aligned fashion brands与同调性的服装品牌设计"服装+香气"共推联名策略
  • Built activation framework connecting sensory experience to brand story构建以感官体验为入口、以品牌故事为核心的活动框架
Outcome
成果
  • Established first distribution and boutique relationships in France and wider Europe建立了在法国及欧洲市场的首批分销与精品店合作关系
  • Brand narrative repositioned within the niche perfumery movement品牌叙事成功嵌入欧洲小众香水运动的语境
  • KOL and press seeding activated organic credibility in fragrance communitiesKOL 与媒体种草在香水圈建立了真实的有机口碑
  • Paris Perfume Week presence created institutional visibility and trade momentum巴黎香水周的亮相建立了机构层面的可见度与贸易势能
Insight
洞察

In the European fragrance market, distribution opens doors — but narrative is what keeps them open. Relationships without a story don't scale.

在欧洲香水市场，渠道能打开门，但只有叙事才能让门保持敞开。没有故事的关系网络无法持续积累势能。

Asian Cinema
European Promotion Strategy
亚洲电影
欧洲推广策略

Designing a structured European promotional strategy for Asian films — combining international film festival presence, offline roadshows, and multi-platform social activation to build both institutional credibility and audience reach.

为亚洲电影设计结构化的欧洲推广策略——以国际电影节为锚点，以线下路演为核心，以多平台社交传播为放大器，同步建立机构公信力与真实受众触达。

Challenge
挑战

Culturally significant films without the institutional infrastructure needed to reach European audiences:

内容质量出众，却缺乏进入欧洲受众视野所需的机构基础设施：

  • No European distribution network or press relationships在欧洲缺乏发行网络与媒体关系
  • Unfamiliarity with European film festival submission and selection dynamics对欧洲电影节报名与评选机制不熟悉
  • Cultural narrative not yet adapted to European audience expectations文化叙事尚未针对欧洲受众进行本地化转译
  • No structured social media strategy for European platforms缺乏面向欧洲平台的系统性社交媒体策略
Approach
方法
1. Film Festival Strategy
1. 电影节参展策略
  • Mapped the European film festival circuit and identified priority submission targets梳理欧洲电影节版图，识别优先报名目标与最佳时间窗口
  • Developed festival submission materials with culturally adapted pitch narratives制作融入本地化叙事的电影节报名材料与项目陈述
  • Designed on-site presence strategy to maximise press and industry contacts设计现场参与策略，最大化媒体曝光与行业人脉积累
2. Offline Roadshow Design
2. 线下路演体系
  • Designed multi-city roadshow format across France, Germany, and Italy设计覆盖法国、德国、意大利的多城市路演格式
  • Partnered with cultural institutions, art-house cinemas, and diaspora communities与文化机构、艺术院线及华人社群建立合作框架
  • Structured Q&A sessions and post-screening events to deepen audience connection设计映后 Q&A 与活动环节，深化观众情感联结
3. Multi-Platform Social Activation
3. 多平台社交传播
  • Built content strategy across Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok for European audiences构建覆盖 Instagram、YouTube 与 TikTok 的欧洲受众内容策略
  • Designed bilingual content formats bridging Asian cultural context and European accessibility设计双语内容格式，连接亚洲文化语境与欧洲受众的可及性
  • Activated film critics, cultural bloggers, and cinephile communities for organic reach激活影评人、文化博主与影迷社群，建立有机传播网络
  • Coordinated social calendar around festival moments and screening milestones以电影节节点与放映里程碑为轴，设计社交内容日历
Outcome
成果
  • Structured film festival submission roadmap with priority targets and adapted materials形成有优先级与本地化材料的电影节报名路线图
  • Multi-city roadshow framework engaging cultural venues and diaspora networks建立覆盖多城市文化场所与华人社群的路演体系
  • Cross-platform social strategy activating European cinephile communities跨平台社交策略成功激活欧洲影迷社群
  • Coherent European narrative positioning Asian cinema as culturally essential, not niche构建将亚洲电影定位为「文化必要」而非「小众特殊」的欧洲叙事框架

洞察

Film travels further when it arrives with institutional credibility. Festivals are not the destination — they are the entry point to the distribution ecosystem.

当电影携带机构公信力抵达时，它能走得更远。电影节不是终点，而是进入发行生态系统的入口。
