01
About
我们是谁

独立，不妥协

Independent, uncompromised

Studio Keer 是一家独立的品牌策略咨询公司。我们不是执行供应商，不是媒介代理，也不是模板提供者。我们只做一件事：帮助品牌在复杂市场中找到清晰的增长路径。

Studio Keer is an independent brand strategy consultancy. We are not an execution vendor, a media agency, or a template provider. We do one thing: help brands find a clear growth path in complex markets.

我们是谁
Who we are

Studio Keer 专注于中国市场进入与跨市场品牌增长。我们服务的品牌，要么正在进入中国，要么正在从中国走向世界。两个方向，一套方法论：从第一性原理出发，在文化落差中建立真实的品牌存在感。

Studio Keer specialises in China market entry and cross-market brand growth. The brands we work with are either entering China, or moving from China into the world. Two directions, one methodology: first-principles thinking, building genuine brand presence across cultural gaps.

我们不做什么
What we don't do

我们不提供通用的执行方案。每个品牌的挑战都不同，复制粘贴的策略只会产生平庸的结果。我们也不在乎"行业最佳实践"——大多数最佳实践都是对过去的总结，而不是对当下的回答。

We don't offer off-the-shelf execution. Every brand's challenge is different, and copy-paste strategy only produces mediocre results. We don't care about "industry best practices" either — most best practices are summaries of the past, not answers for the present.

02
Perspective
视角

Different markets require
different logics.

不同市场需要
不同的逻辑。

Platforms are not interchangeable
平台不可互换

Each market has its own user psychology. What works on Instagram fails on Xiaohongshu. What converts on TikTok doesn't on Douyin.

每个市场都有自己的用户心理。Instagram 上有效的方式在小红书上会失败。TikTok 上能转化的内容在抖音上不行。

Strategy is not transferable
策略不可平移

What works in one context often fails in another. A brand that succeeds in Europe with one approach needs a fundamentally different one for China.

在一个市场有效的方法往往在另一个市场失效。在欧洲成功的品牌策略，进入中国需要根本性的重构。

Growth requires adaptation
增长需要适配

Growth comes from adaptation, not replication. Brands that try to copy their domestic playbook into new markets produce mediocre results.

增长来自适配，而非复制。将国内打法照搬到新市场的品牌，只会得到平庸的结果。
